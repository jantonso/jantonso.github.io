Canvass

A hyper-localized, anonymous Q&A app

Canvass is a location based social app to express yourself freely and to gain statistical insights into the thoughts & opinions of your community. Through asking and answering open-ended, multiple choice, and yes/no questions you can gage the aggregated opinion of the people around you.

To date, Canvass has been downloaded by over 1,700 users, who asked nearly 9,300 questions, responded with over 214,000 answers, voiced 5,500 comments, and upvoted/downvoted content more than 28,000 times.

Tech Stack

Canvass is a cross-platform mobile app built using the Ionic framework and AngularJS. We used Adobe Phonegap Build for the build process and OneSignal for multi-platform push notifications.

For the backend, our REST API was built in Java on Google App Engine. We used Google Cloud SQL as our datastore.

More Information

You can find the app in the IOS Store and in the Google Play Store for Android.
