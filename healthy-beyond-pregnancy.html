Healthy Beyond Pregnancy

Web Service for Postpartum Care and Scheduling

I consulted with a group of researchers at the Magee's Women's Hospital of UPMC for six months in order to create this project. Healthy Beyond Pregnancy provides patients with critical postpartum information and the ability to schedule a follow up appointment to check on their progress post pregnancy.

Functional Requirements

  1. Patient answers a list of postpartum questions

  2. Patient watches videos with information tailored to their needs

  3. Patient schedules follow-up appointments 4-6 weeks from date of delivery

  4. Patient provides contact information and signs consent form

  5. Patient receives text messages with updates as appointment nears

  6. Patient's information and answers can be printed easily at the end of the process via PDF format

  7. All information is recorded and stored in a database

  8. Website must be responsive for a variety of tablets

Architecture

This project was built using Django and is currently hosted on Heroku w/ a PostgreSQL DB.

Uses signature_pad for HTML5 canvass based signature forms, jQuery UI's Datepicker for the custom calendar interface, and Python's ReportLab for generating PDFs.
